# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Logo & Homepage Design

This project was created as part of the **Logo Design and Website Development assignment** for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**.

The goal was to design a professional brand identity and build a responsive homepage that represents a modern institute focused on **digital risk, cyber security, and AI governance**.

### Logo Design Explanation

The logo uses a **cube-inspired geometric structure** to represent digital systems, structure, and risk management. The cube symbolizes **stability and resilience** in complex digital environments. The **orange, white, and black** color palette reflects **energy, innovation, and professionalism**. The minimalist design ensures the logo remains clear and recognizable even at smaller sizes such as mobile headers or favicons.
