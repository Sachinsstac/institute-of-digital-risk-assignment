About IDR

Bridging the Gap Between Knowledge & Practice

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led institute dedicated to training and deploying the next generation of professionals in digital risk, cyber security, and AI governance. We believe that managing digital risk is one of the most critical challenges of our time.

Through deep collaboration between academia and industry partners, IDR delivers cutting-edge programs that combine rigorous academic foundations with real-world practical experience. Our graduates are equipped to tackle the evolving landscape of digital threats, regulatory requirements, and emerging technology risks across sectors including financial services, healthcare, and critical infrastructure.
